1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Nexora Cloud:

Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account InfoName, email address (from Google OAuth)To identify your account
FilesFiles and scripts you uploadTo run your bots and services
Activity LogsActions performed (uploads, starts, stops)Security and debugging
Session DataEncrypted session tokensTo keep you logged in
Support MessagesMessages sent to supportTo respond to your requests

We do not collect your passwords (Google handles authentication), payment card numbers (payments processed by third parties), or any biometric data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Nexora Cloud service
  • Process your uploads and execute your scripts
  • Communicate with you about your account (via platform notifications)
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and investigate violations
  • Monitor and improve platform security
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database (by Google), which provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

File content is stored as base64-encoded data and is only accessible to you and administrators of the platform.

Session tokens are generated with cryptographically secure randomness and expire after 30 days of inactivity.

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong Google account password and enable two-factor authentication on your Google account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google for data storage and authentication
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Nexora Cloud, our users, or others
  • Sub-Users: If you grant sub-user access, that person can see your files and activity per the permissions you set

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your session ID (nsid) which keeps you logged in. This is strictly necessary for the service to function and is not used for tracking or advertising.

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — View all data associated with your account
  • Delete — Request deletion of your account and all associated data from Settings
  • Export — Download your files at any time from the File Manager
  • Correct — Update your display name through Settings

To exercise these rights, use the Settings page or contact us via Support.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Account information is permanently deleted immediately
  • Files and file content are permanently deleted immediately
  • Activity logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security purposes

Note: If an administrator deletes your account with a re-registration cooldown, a record of your email address is kept until the cooldown expires, after which it is automatically purged.

8. Children's Privacy

Nexora Cloud is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through the Support page within the platform.

We will respond to privacy-related requests within 30 days.
